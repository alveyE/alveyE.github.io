The Next Step in Mobile Communication

Waypoint Communication is a location based social media app for travelers, friends, businesses, groups, and just about everyone.Upload your favorite memories, that awesome wildlife shot, business information, or anything else!

Waypoint Communication will be available soon for all modern iOS devices and later for Android devices.

Using Waypoint Communication is Easy

Easily See All Notes Around You

The Waypoint Communication Map shows all the recent notes posted around you. Using the explore tab, you can find all the interesting notes you like!
Add Photos, Text, Links, and Drawings

Post your own notes with pictures, text, links, and drawings. Just add a title and all the information you want and post the note to your current location!
Bookmark Interesting Notes For Later

Find an interesting note? Bookmark it for later and see it whenever you want. You can find all your notes in the bookmark tab!
