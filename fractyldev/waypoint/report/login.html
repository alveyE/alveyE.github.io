Safety and Acceptance are Our
#1 Priorities.

Waypoint Communications and the Fractyl Development team are committed to the safety and well being of our users.

This is why we let all users report any note that violates the privacy policy or terms of service. This can include, but is not limited to:


  • Cyberbulling and harrassment
  • Sharing Vulgar or inappropriate language and/or content
  • Sharing anyone's personal information or data
  • Defaming or slandering someone or something

You can report notes on the Waypoint app today.
